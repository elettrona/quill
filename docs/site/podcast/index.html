The QUILL Cast

A two-host audio course on QUILL, the free, screen-reader-first writing studio from Community Access. Liam and Jessica walk through every major concept: navigation, documents and formats, document rescue and OCR, the private speech suite, optional AI, the Accessible Vault, Story Studio, the GLOW accessibility review system, and the trust architecture that holds it all together. Episode voices are generated with QUILL's own on-device Kokoro neural speech engine.

Root-cause theme: status confirmations are silent under a screen reader

_set_status(msg) is the standard way commands report their result. It updates the status-bar text and calls the announcement engine, but the engine deliberately stays silent while JAWS/NVDA is active (prism_bridge.py:303-314) to avoid talking over the screen reader – on the assumption the screen reader will read whatever focus/control change the action caused. Commands that change nothing focus-wise (an indent, a “no changes” check, a “nothing found” result) therefore produce a visible status the user never hears. The Tab-indent fix (item 3) used the force=True escape hatch. The same pattern explains items 9 and 13, and likely contributes to 10. A small systemic fix is proposed below.

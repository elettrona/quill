Modal dialogs deserve a minute, because QUILL holds them to a written contract. Every dialog in the product is tracked in an internal inventory and must meet rules: reachable by keyboard, escapable with escape, focus placed sensibly on open and restored on close. There's literally an automated gate in QUILL's development process that fails the build if a dialog breaks the contract. You'll feel this as an absence, the absence of ever being trapped.
